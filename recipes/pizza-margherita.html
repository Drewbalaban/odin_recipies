Pizza Margherita

The classic Italian pizza with fresh mozzarella, basil, and tomato sauce

Description

Pizza Margherita is the quintessential Italian pizza, named after Queen Margherita of Savoy. This simple yet perfect pizza features the colors of the Italian flag: red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil.

The key to an authentic Margherita pizza is using the highest quality ingredients - San Marzano tomatoes, fresh buffalo mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. The dough should be thin and crispy with a slightly charred crust from a hot oven.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (San Marzano tomatoes preferred)
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, yeast, and salt. Gradually add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  3. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover with a towel, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 500¬∞F (260¬∞C) with a pizza stone if available.
  5. Punch down dough and divide into 2 portions. Roll each into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Spread tomato sauce evenly over each pizza, leaving a 1-inch border for the crust.
  7. Arrange mozzarella slices over the sauce.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven and immediately add fresh basil leaves.
  10. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  11. Let rest for 2-3 minutes before slicing and serving.

Cooking Tips

  • Use a pizza stone or steel for the best crust results
  • Don't overload the pizza with toppings - less is more
  • Fresh mozzarella should be patted dry before using
  • Add basil after baking to preserve its fresh flavor
  • Let the dough rest at room temperature for 30 minutes before shaping
