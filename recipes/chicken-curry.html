Chicken Curry

Description

This aromatic chicken curry is a perfect blend of spices and tender chicken pieces simmered in a rich, flavorful sauce. It's a comforting dish that brings warmth and depth to any meal.

The curry features a perfect balance of heat and flavor, making it suitable for both spice lovers and those who prefer milder dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 (14-ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add curry powder, turmeric, and cumin. Stir for 30 seconds to toast the spices.
  5. Add chicken cubes and cook until lightly browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  6. Pour in coconut milk and diced tomatoes. Stir in tomato paste.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low.
  9. Cover and cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Cook until chicken is tender and sauce has thickened.
  11. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
  12. Serve hot over cooked rice.
